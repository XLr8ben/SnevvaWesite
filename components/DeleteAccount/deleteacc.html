⏱️

Deletion timeline

After your request is submitted, here's what happens:

1

Confirmation email sent immediately

We'll email a confirmation to your registered address. Your account is deactivated and no longer accessible.

2

30-day grace period

Your data is preserved for 30 days in case you change your mind. Log back in to cancel the deletion anytime during this window.

3

Permanent deletion on Day 30

All personal health data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our primary servers.

4

Backup purge within 90 days

Any remaining copies in encrypted backup systems are also purged within 90 days of the deletion request.

💡

Before you go — alternatives

Account deletion is permanent. Consider one of these options first:

⏸️

Pause your account

Stop all syncing and notifications without losing your data.
📤

Export your data

Download a full copy of your health history before leaving.
🔔

Manage notifications

Too many reminders? Customise or turn them all off easily.
💬

Talk to support

We'd love to help fix whatever's not working for you.
