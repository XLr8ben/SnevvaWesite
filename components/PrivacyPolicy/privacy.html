At Snevva, your health data is among the most personal information you could share with us. We treat it with the care, respect, and discretion it deserves. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the control you have over it — in plain language, not legalese.

👋

Overview

Snevva Health Technologies ("Snevva", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Snevva mobile application and related services. This policy describes our practices when you use our app, website, or any other Snevva-branded service.

By using Snevva, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you disagree with any part, please discontinue use and contact us so we can understand your concerns.

🛡️
Our commitment: We never sell your personal health data to third parties. Your information is used only to provide and improve the Snevva experience for you.
📋

Data We Collect

We collect information to deliver a personalised, intelligent health companion experience. Here's a clear breakdown of every category:

Category Examples Required?
Account info Name, email, date of birth, password (hashed) Yes
Health metrics Steps, sleep duration, heart rate, weight, water intake Optional
Nutrition logs Meal entries, calorie counts, macros Optional
Mood & wellness Daily mood check-ins, stress levels, journal entries Optional
Women's health Cycle dates, flow intensity, symptoms Optional
Medication logs Medication names, dosage, timing Optional
Device data Device model, OS version, app version, crash logs Automatic
Usage analytics Features used, session duration (anonymised) Automatic
💡
You control optional data. Every health module in Snevva is opt-in. You can use the app without logging your cycle, mood, medications, or any other sensitive category.
⚙️

How We Use Your Data

Every use of your data has a clear, user-focused purpose. We use your information to:

  • Deliver your personalised health dashboard and daily wellness score
  • Generate AI insights and trend analysis based on your logged metrics
  • Send smart reminders for medications, hydration, and habits
  • Identify patterns across sleep, mood, nutrition, and activity
  • Improve and debug the Snevva app (using anonymised data)
  • Send account-related notifications (e.g. password resets, policy updates)
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations where required
⚠️
We do not use your health data for targeted advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or any purpose beyond delivering the Snevva experience to you.
🤝

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal health data. Limited sharing occurs only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted infrastructure partners (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics) who are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for Snevva services.
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user data may transfer to the successor — you will be notified in advance.
  • With your explicit consent: For example, if you choose to share a health report with a healthcare provider via Snevva's export feature.
🔐

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard protections:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Health data is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Access to production systems is restricted and logged
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot read them

Data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days (backups within 90 days). See our account deletion guide for details.

Cookies

Our website uses a minimal set of cookies. The mobile app does not use browser cookies.

  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication and session management. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymised usage data to help us improve the website. You may opt out.
  • Preference cookies: Remembers your theme or language preference.

You can manage cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Snevva is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately at privacy@snevva.com and we will promptly delete it.
